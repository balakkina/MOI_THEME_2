BP-TS-01 - Application for Major Building Permit (new / renew /add / heightening / modification / temporary cease / cancelation of cease)

  •  New
  •  Renew
  •  Heightening
  •  Modification
  •  Add
Process Name Application for Major Building Permit (New)
Process ID BP-TS-01
Process Description A building license issued by the municipality according to its approved documents and drawing.
Application Processing
  1. Fill out the permit application including all the data of the owner, plot and design consultant by the applicant (design consultant).
  2. Attach required document.
  3. Payment of the application fees.
  4. Notification of the beneficiary for the application acceptance or Complete the notes if find.
  5. Notify the beneficiary of the technical inspection appointment date if is required.
  6. Pay fees.
  7. Issuance of the license and payment of the fees.
Required Documents
  1. Title Deed
  2. Krooky
  3. Detailed Maps of Architectural Schemas
  4. Detailed Maps of Structural Schemas
  5. Approvals (Omantel/Ooredo/PAEW)
  6. PACDA Approved Maps
  7. SOIL Test Report
  8. Govt.Bodies Approval
  9. Stability Certificate
  10. Design Calculation book
Process Duration From 3 to 10 official working days
Fee Application fee OMR 30
Service Fee OMR 70
Resubmission Fee OMR 5
Process Name Extraction of a major permit to build additional floors on an existing building not previously issued a building permit. (Renew)
Process ID BP-TS-01
Process Description A building license issued by the municipality according to its approved documents and drawing to build additional floors on an existing building not previously issued a building permit
Application Processing
  1. Fill out the permit application including all the data of the owner, plot and design consultant by the applicant (design consultant).
  2. Attach required document.
  3. Payment of the application fees.
  4. Notification of the beneficiary for the application acceptance or Complete the notes if find.
  5. Notify the beneficiary for the date of the visit to conduct the technical inspection of the site.
  6. Conducting the technical inspection of the required building by the building technician and preparing the technical report with the attached photographs of the site showing its features.
  7. Pay fees.
  8. Issuance the license.
Required Documents
  1. Previously approved permits and maps.
  2. A copy of the title deed and the survey.
  3. Electronic version (PDF) including architectural and construction drawings (detailed building drawings For the existing building and construction to be constructed certified by the consulting office design) as well as a booklet of construction accounts.
  4. Approved maps from the General Authority for Civil Defense and Aid for governmental, industrial and tourist buildings, residential and commercial residential and tourist buildings, wedding halls and educational buildings with a floor area of 350 square meters or the ground floor. Commercial warehouses The area of the shop exceeds 60 square meters.
  5. Attach a report from the supervising consultant that the building bears the required floors.
  6. Consultant designed report to Endure that building required.
  7. Additional approvals from some government agencies according to the nature of the project (Ministry of Housing, General Electricity and Water Authority, Royal Oman Police, Ministry of Tourism, Ministry of Environment and Climate Affairs, Ministry of Heritage and Culture, Ministry of Awqaf and Religious Affairs.) And any other approval required by the Municipality.
Process Duration From 3 to 10 official working days
Fee Application fee OMR 30
Service Fee OMR 70
Resubmission Fee OMR 5

Process Name Demolition Permit
Process ID BP-TS-13-2
Process Description A demolition permit issued by the municipality to carry out the demolitions according to the documents.

BP-TS-04 - Application for Construction Permit

Process Name Application for Construction Permit Apply
Process ID BP-TS-04
Application Processing
  1. Fill out the construction starting application including all the data of the owner, plot and design consultant by the applicant.
  2. Attach required document.
  3. Paymen t of the application fees.
  4. Notification of the beneficiary for the acceptance of the application or Complete the notes if find.
  5. Notify the beneficiary for the date of the visit to conduct the technical inspection of the site.
  6. Conducting the technical inspection of the site to make sure the plot is fenced according to the specifications and the project board according to the prepared model prior to implementation in the case of the new projects.
  7. Contractor's deposit cash or bank guarantee (2%) of the value of the works as insurance for the removal of the waste (depending on the building area required).
  8. Pay fees.
  9. Issue the license.
    10. Notes:
    • The implementation follow-up report shall be submitted by the consultant prior to the commencement of any stage of construction listed in the report. The inspection shall be carried out by the municipality and the report shall be approved.
    • The consultant is required to supervise the buildings that cost more than (RO 10,000 - 10,000).
Required Documents
  1. A copy of the municipal license and the certificate of affiliation to the Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Oman and valid commercial registers of the contractor and the supervising consultant.
  2. Attach the undertaking of the implementing contractor and the supervising consultant.
  3. A certified copy of the electricity plan from the electricity distributing company in the region.
  4. Undertaking of colors (signed and stamped by the owner, the contractor and the consultant).
  5. The plot marks delivery form for the space lands without fence that has not been received for more than two years.
Process Duration From 1 to 3 official working days
Fee Application fee OMR 30
Service Fee OMR 70
Resubmission Fee OMR 25

BP-TS-05 - Consultant Reports for Buildings Implementation Follow-up

Process Name Consultant Reports for Buildings Implementation Follow-up
Process ID BP-TS-05
Application Processing
  1. The ministry receives the building permit application form with the owner details, land details and consultant details and all required documents attached.
  2. Schedules the appointment for the field visit to carry out technical inspection of the site.
  3. Conducts the technical inspection and prepare the technical report for the site with the site photos attached clarifying its features.
  4. Approves the permit and detailed maps by its engineers.
  5. The required fees are paid by the applicant and the application is transferred to the municipality director for approval.
  6. Issues the building permit to be accessed by the applicant in the portal and a copy of permit and maps is kept in the municipality.
  7. The applicant is notified with the process steps, additional required documents or clarifications needed with SMS and Email services.
Required Documents
Process Duration 3 to 10 working days
