Required Documents

Title Deed

Title Deed

Krooky

Krooky

Architectural Schemas

Copy of the architectural schemas (Detailed maps of the building certified by the designing consultant office in addition to the structural calculations booklet.

Structural Schemas – Foundation Layout

Copy of the structural schemas (Detailed maps of the building certified by the designing consultant office in addition to the structural calculations booklet.

Approvals (Omantel/Ooredo/PAEW)

Approvals from concerned authorities (Omantel , Ooredoo,PAEW)

PACDA Approved Maps

Approval of maps by the General Authority of Civil Defense and Ambulance for governmental, industrial and tourism buildings, residential, residential commercial and tourism buildings, weddings halls and educational buildings where the area of any floor exceeds (350) square meters, or the ground floor used for commercial purposes and one shop area of more than (60) square meters.

SOIL Test Report

Provide soil testing report from one of the specialized laboratories (within Sultanate) for buildings, where height exceeds three floors. Soil testing report provided by the designer consultant will be sufficient if the number of floors are three or less.

Govt.Bodies Approval

Approvals of government bodies according to the nature of the project (Ministry of Housing, Civil Defense, Ministry of Tourism, Ministry of Environment and Climate Affairs, Ministry of Heritage and Culture, Ministry of Endowments and Religious Affairs)

Stability Certificate

Approvals of government bodies according to the nature of the project (Ministry of Housing, Civil Defense, Ministry of Tourism, Ministry of Environment and Climate Affairs, Ministry of Heritage and Culture, Ministry of Endowments and Religious Affairs)

Design Calculation book
